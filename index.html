Welcome to Dr. Vandana B Patil's Research Group

Welcome to the Research Group at D Y Patil International University, led by Dr. Vandana B Patil. Our group is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of nanotechnology through innovative research in nano-hetero-architectures and 2D materials.

We focus on developing novel functional materials and devices with controlled features on the nanometer scale, particularly targeting applications in green and sustainable energy sectors. Our research spans across various domains including energy storage, conversion applications, supercapacitors, batteries, and photoelectrochemical water splitting.

Research Interest

Core Research Areas

Dr. Patil's research focuses on the synthesis and characterization of nanostructured materials, including Tungsten Trioxide (WO) nanostructures, for gas sensing and supercapacitor applications. Her work also extends to the development of high-performance semiconductor devices, such as lead tungstate (PbWO) nanorod field-effect transistors. The primary research thrust areas include:

  • Material Science & Nanomaterial Synthesis
  • Gas sensors and Supercapacitor Devices
  • Graphene Synthesis & Batteries
  • Renewable energy sources & Piezoelectric Materials
  • Thin Films, Solar Cells, and Transparent conductive oxides
  • Photoelectrochemistry & Photo-electrocatalysis

    • This project was selected for the state-level Avishkar competition organized by SPPU in 2014.

Group Leader

Dr. Vandana B Patil, M.Sc., B.Ed., Ph.D.

Position: Assistant Professor
Department: Physics
Institution: D Y Patil International University, Pune

Dr. Vandana B Patil received her B.Sc. in Physics (2005), M.Sc. in Solid State Physics (2007), B.Ed. (2009), and Ph.D. in Solid State Physics (2017). Her doctoral thesis, awarded on August 3rd, 2017, is titled Synthesis and characterization of Tungsten Trioxide nanostructures by chemical method for gas sensing and supercapacitor applicatons, under the guidance of Dr. S. S. Suryavanshi and co-guidance of Dr. Imtiaz Mulla. Currently, she is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Physics, D. Y. Patil International University, Akurdi, Pune. She previously served as the Head of Innovation and Incubation at Dr. D. Y. Patil Institute of Engineering Management and Research. She has published more than 12 research articles in international journals, has 196 citations and an h-index of 8 on Google Scholar. Dr. Patil has been recognized with numerous awards, including the "Best Guide Award" by Marathi Vidyan Parishad , "Best Teacher Award" from DYPIEMR, and the "Eklawya Scholarship" during her M.Sc.. She holds lifetime memberships with ISTE and the Marathi Vidnyan Parishad. She has been granted 4 Patents and 1 Copyright.
